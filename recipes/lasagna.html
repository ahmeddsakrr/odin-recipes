Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of tender pasta, flavorful meat or vegetarian filling, creamy cheese, and savory tomato sauce. To make lasagna, cook lasagna noodles until al dente and prepare a meat or vegetarian sauce with ingredients like ground beef, Italian sausage, vegetables, herbs, and tomato sauce. Layer the cooked noodles with the sauce, ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, and then bake it in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. The result is a mouthwatering lasagna with a perfect blend of textures and flavors, making it a favorite for gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • Ground beef or saItalianusage (for meat filling) or mixed vegetables (for vegetarian filling)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes
  • Dried herbs like oregano, basil, thyme, and rosemary
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Prepare the meat or vegetarian filling with your choice of ingredients.
  3. Layer the cooked noodles with the sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled.
  5. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
