Meatballs Recipe

Description

Meatballs are a classic dish made from ground meat mixed with various seasonings and ingredients. They are typically shaped into small balls and cooked until browned and flavorful. Meatballs can be made with different types of meat such as beef, pork, chicken, or a combination of meats. The cooked meatballs are often served with pasta, tomato sauce, or in sandwiches, and they make a delicious and versatile meal option.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat (beef, pork, chicken, or a combination)
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Onion and garlic (minced)
  • Herbs and spices (such as parsley, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper)
  • Milk (optional, for added moisture)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground meat, breadcrumbs, minced onion, minced garlic, herbs, spices, and beaten egg.
  2. Mix the ingredients together until well combined. If the mixture appears dry, you can add a small amount of milk to moisten it.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls, approximately 1-2 inches in diameter, using your hands.
  4. Heat a pan with some oil over medium heat. Place the meatballs in the pan and cook until browned on all sides, ensuring they are cooked through.
  5. Once cooked, you can serve the meatballs with your choice of accompaniment, such as pasta, tomato sauce, or in a sandwich. Enjoy!
